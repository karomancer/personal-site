Choropleth Growth Map
Visualizing (internet) growth in 30 seconds
Data Engineering & Web Development, 2012
introduction
While I was interning at Yammer Inc in 2012, I took part in one of the company's famous hackathons. For this hackathon, I worked on two projects, and as a data engineer intern, it only made sense to have one revolve around displaying data.

With a team of data analysts, I created this choropleth growth map for visualizing growing global sales in Yammer Inc in just 24 hours. However, to display to you today, I fed in World Bank data on internet growth since 1990.

This project is easily adaptable to JSON data sets with country and numerical information. It was written in Javascript using the d3 library.

Timbeleza
Spreading the Brazilian Samba Spirit
Graphic Design, 2010
introduction
A few years back, a friend of mine local to Pittsburgh passed my name around for freelance work. I heard back from an amazing Brazilian styled band called Timbeleza.

Timbeleza is an all percussion samba band which, at the time, was preparing to go on an international tour. For this tour they wanted to revamp the designs on their T-shirts and posters, and thus I was hired to work on a design that incorporated the colors of the Brazilian flag, the energy of samba dancers, and the ferocity of a helicopter.

The designs went through many iterations, many of which I had gotten carried away with in complexity and colors. We finally agreed on a design that the group thought best represented its spirit and members, and looked snazzy enough to put on shirts and posters.

sketching & ideation
I started my process by sketching out lively, Brazilian themes. Timbeleza has a couple samba dancers in the group, so I looked up colorful samba costumes and the different instruments found in a samba band for inspiration. I oscillated between instrument-centric and dance-centric designs until I finally created the perfect design for Timbeleza.
final product

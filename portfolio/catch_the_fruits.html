Catch the Fruits (in progress)

A very good friend of mine from high school, Lincoln Race, studies computer science and astrophysics at University of California San Diego. He also is very interested in mobile game development, and asked for my assistance in producing the graphics for his game Catch the Fruit.

Catch the Fruits is an Android game, available for free, on the Google Play Store. The official desciprtion goes as follows: "In Catch the Fruits, you'll be able to use a touch controlled basket to catch, well, fruits. with 7 currently planned regions and over 50 fruits, there's plenty of content to unlock, all completely free if you're good enough or if you share this app with your Facebook friends via the game."
